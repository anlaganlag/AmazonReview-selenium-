  This juicer was a present for my sister, my second home :). Our tea-time-chats have become juice-time-chats, thanks to this nifty juicer!<br/>Operating it is pretty easy. Add fruits and veggies to chute, there are 2 speed settings for soft and hard textures, push and fresh juice comes pouring out of the spout.<br/>Clean up is breeze. There are 5 main parts that need to be rinsed, but since assembly and dissembly is pretty quick, it makes cleanup quicker too.<br/>If they could just make the juice container a lil bigger that would be awesome. It works for small batches like 2 of us, but for bigger batches like 4-5 ppl, the container needs to be emptied in the middle else it will overflow.<br/>Overall, its a good electric juicer given the price.

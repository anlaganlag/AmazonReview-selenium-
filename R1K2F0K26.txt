After tinkering with it I was able to assemble all but one piece pictured. I made my first juice consisting of carrots and apples. Well the carrots shot like a firecracker when I pushed the plunger. Scared me half to death. I decided to go slow and it was ok. I don&#8217;t think the lid broke but it may not survive another hard push. The apples were much easier and not as traumatic. The juice came out delicious with quite a bit of liquid compared to an older one I was using last week. Not a lot of foam either. Clean up wasn&#8217;t so bad. Love the brush it comes with.  I will keep it until it breaks. I&#8217;m not sure it will survive another carrot.
